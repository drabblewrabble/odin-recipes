Tortilla de Patata

Description

The tortilla de patata is a lovely brunch or anytime dish that's simple to make. I would argue that nothing is more fun than chomping on some onions, potates, cheese, and egg. Yummy!

Supplies

  • Cooking Oil of Choice
  • Eggs
  • Little Potato Guys
  • ONIONS

Steps On Me Guide

  1. PREP WORK

    Dice the potates into little pyramid shapes, about the size of a monopoly token...

    Dice the onions and mix with the potato pyamids.

    Combine the potato guys and onion boys in a bowl and set aside.

  2. Heat up a (preferably nonstick or enamel) pan on medium heat with cooking oil.
  3. Crack just like a ton of eggs into a jar or something and shake it up until beaten.
  4. Mix the beaten eggs with the potato and onion guys in their bowl home.
  5. Thickly layer that in the preheated and oiled pan and wait until and undercrust forms
  6. With the mission impossible theme playing, flip that queen and cook through on the other side.
  7. Bust out the toppings of choice, I love curry ketchup for this recipe just to piss off the masses...

Up TOP!
